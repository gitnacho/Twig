``date_modify``
===============

.. versionadded:: 1.9.0
    El filtro ``date_modify`` se añadió en *Twig* 1.9.0.

El filtro ``date_modify`` modifica una fecha con una cadena de caracteres modificadores suministrada:

.. code-block:: jinja

    {{ post.published_at|date_modify("+1 day")|date("m/d/Y") }}

The ``date_modify`` filter accepts strings (it must be in a format supported
by the `strtotime`_ function) or `DateTime`_ instances. You can easily combine
it with the :doc:`date<date>` filter for formatting.

Argumentos
----------

 * ``modifier``: The modifier

.. _`strtotime`: http://www.php.net/strtotime
.. _`DateTime`:  http://www.php.net/DateTime
